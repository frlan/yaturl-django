F.A.Q. — Frequently Asked Questions

Which data are stored of my request?

We are not storing personal data.

In detail these are for inserting a link:

  • Entry time

For accessing a link:

  • Access time
  • Accessed link
  • Used browser (user-agent, via logfiles)

We do not store any IP-addresses or other personal data.

How to donate?

You can donate in many different ways:

  • Use the service and spread the world
  • Give feedback
  • Create patches for features or bugs if you can do so
